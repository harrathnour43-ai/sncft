Our Story

A journey of progress and national development

Founded in 1956, the year of Tunisia's independence, SNCFT has been the backbone of the nation's transportation infrastructure for over six decades. From our humble beginnings with just a few dozen locomotives to today's modern fleet, we have grown alongside our nation.

Throughout our history, we have faced challenges and embraced innovation, always striving to provide the Tunisian people with safe, reliable, and comfortable railway transportation. Our journey reflects Tunisia's own path of development and modernization.

Today, SNCFT stands as a symbol of national pride, connecting cities, communities, and cultures across the beautiful landscape of Tunisia, from the Mediterranean coast to the Sahara desert.

Sustainability Commitment

Building a greener future for Tunisia

At SNCFT, we are deeply committed to environmental sustainability and reducing our carbon footprint. Railway transportation is inherently one of the most environmentally friendly modes of transport, and we continuously work to enhance our green credentials.

Our sustainability initiatives include energy-efficient locomotives, waste reduction programs, renewable energy adoption, and partnerships with environmental organizations to preserve Tunisia's natural beauty for future generations.
